Banke National Park

Lumbini Province, Nepal

Nepal's tenth national park and a key Tiger Conservation Unit, spanning 550 km² of Terai Sal forests, Churia ranges and the floodplains of the Rapti and Babai rivers — home to Bengal tigers, four-horned antelope, striped hyenas, gharial crocodiles and over 250 species of birds.
